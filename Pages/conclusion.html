Our Conclusion
What we learned.

Upon undertaking the task of finishing an effective parking app where the former team had left off, we quickly realized that, with the time and resources available to us, having a deployable app by the end of the semester was simply not possible The direction the former team had went with the solution was simply not feasible. We discovered that there were too many variables to overcome in using cameras with video recognition software and that there were simpler solutions available.

  Considering the 180 this project needed to take in order to be feasible, it set the time schedule back significantly considering all the work of the previous team was not usable.
